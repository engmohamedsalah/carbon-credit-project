Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is Carbon Credit Verification SaaS?

Carbon Credit Verification SaaS is a software-as-a-service platform that uses satellite imagery, machine learning, and blockchain technology to verify carbon credits from forestry projects.

How does the verification process work?

The verification process involves analyzing satellite imagery to detect forest cover changes, calculating carbon sequestration, and certifying the results on the blockchain. The process includes human expert review to ensure accuracy.

What types of projects can be verified?

Currently, the platform focuses on forestry projects, including forest conservation, reforestation, and afforestation. Future updates will expand to other project types.

Technical Questions

What satellite imagery is used?

The platform primarily uses Sentinel-2 multispectral imagery with 10m spatial resolution, which provides excellent data for vegetation analysis.

How accurate is the AI model?

The AI model achieves over 90% accuracy in forest cover change detection, with continuous improvements through human-in-the-loop feedback.

Is the platform secure?

Yes, the platform implements industry-standard security measures, including encryption, secure authentication, and regular security audits.

Blockchain Questions

Why use blockchain for certification?

Blockchain provides an immutable, transparent record of verification results, preventing double-counting and ensuring the integrity of carbon credits.

Which blockchain does the platform use?

The platform uses Polygon, an energy-efficient Layer 2 solution for Ethereum, to minimize the environmental impact of blockchain operations.

How can I verify a certificate?

Each certificate includes a unique blockchain transaction ID that can be verified on the Polygon blockchain explorer.
